# PRD: Sexual Health Education

## Overview

AI-generated education content for all genders: AI podcasts (ElevenLabs dual voice), AI phone calls (quick Q&A), articles/guides, quizzes. Topics: anatomy, pleasure, STI prevention, mental health, relationships, kink safety, LGBTQ+, aging. Content generated dynamically in user's language.

## Target Audience

All users

## Functional Requirements (FR)

### FR-1: AI Podcast Generation
- Priority: Should
- Acceptance criteria: Given a topic, then an AI-generated podcast episode with two voices is produced and playable

### FR-2: AI Phone Calls
- Priority: Should
- Acceptance criteria: Given a user question, then they can call an AI educator for a 5-minute conversation

### FR-3: Articles & Guides
- Priority: Must
- Acceptance criteria: Given topic categories, then AI-generated articles are available, tagged by topic and audience

### FR-4: Quizzes
- Priority: Should
- Acceptance criteria: Given a topic, then interactive quizzes test understanding

## MVP Scope

FR-3 (pre-generated articles for top 20 topics) is MVP.

## Risks and Dependencies

- Depends on F15 (coach engine for voice AI)
- ElevenLabs cost per podcast episode: $1.80-4.50
- Must include medical disclaimer
